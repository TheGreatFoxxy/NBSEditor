About NBSEditor

History

NBSEditor was written in 2018 by GarboMuffin (Thomas Weber). I aquired a copy in late 2019, as a largely abandoned project. I then started rebuilding, refactoring, and reshaping the app once known as nbsplayer. It is now what it is today!

Some hacks

While using the app myself, I've discovered some hacks. Here are some of them!

  • Zooming out does help with how many blocks can fit into the screen, and how many layers on the side
  • If the startup sound dosent play, click the red refresh page button, and it will play! (maybe. its kinda intermittent)
  • Recording with a screen recorder does, and will capture the audio in very high quality, depending on your screen recorder
